I am currently an Assistant Researcher in Zhongguancun Laboratory. I received my bachelor's degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in 2020, luckily supervised by [Prof. Hang Su](https://www.suhangss.me/) and [Prof. Jun Zhu](https://ml.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/~jun/index.shtml). I received my Ph.D. from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2025, luckily supervised by [Prof. Shiming Ge](https://imsg.ac.cn/people/geshiming.html) and [Prof. Can Ma](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~macan), with a joint Ph.D. program in EFPL from 2023 to 2024, luckily supervised by [Prof. Tong Zhang](https://sites.google.com/view/tong-zhang) and [Prof. Sabine Süsstrunk](https://people.epfl.ch/sabine.susstrunk?lang=en).

Previously, I was a Research Intern in Huawei Noah's Ark Lab (2025.4-2025.7), luckily supervised by [Dr. Feng Tang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=GC2xivIAAAAJ) and [Dr. Zhongang Qi](https://zhongangqi.github.io/). I was a Research Intern in RealAI (2022.10-2023.7), luckily supervised by [Zihao Xiao](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=fOfxmrcAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao) and [Prof. Jianmin Li](https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/csen/info/1154/3991.htm). I also feel lucky to have the chance to collaborate with [Dr. Jianmin Bao](https://jianminbao.github.io/) from Microsoft Research Asia durin my Ph.D. More previously, I was an intern in Tencent TEG (2019.6-2019.9) and Ant Group (2019.10-2020.6).

Currently, my research focuses on Computer Vision, Multimodal Media Forensics, and AIGC/LLM/AI Safety, including the attack and defense of generative models (such as Large Language Models and Diffusion Models) and how to detect generated and manipulated content.
If you are also interested in similar topics or seeking any form of research collaboration, please feel free to reach out to me by email. I always enjoy working with excellent researchers!
